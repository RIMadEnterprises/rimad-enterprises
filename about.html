Company Story

RIMad Enterprises began as a vision to combine practical construction know-how with contemporary business systems. Founded in Kampala, the company has steadily grown into a trusted multi-business enterprise operating across real estate, construction, technology services, and catering. Our projects focus on sustainability, value creation, and community uplift.

Vision

To be a leading African enterprise recognized for transforming industries, improving lives, and inspiring progress across generations.

Mission

To build sustainable enterprises that empower communities and drive economic growth through innovation, integrity, and excellence in every sector we serve.
